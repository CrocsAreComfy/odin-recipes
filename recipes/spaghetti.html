Spaghetti

Description

This recipe for the spaghetti red dates back to the days of old from Siciliy. Reminicent of an old family secret dish, this recipe will have your mouth watering. The tantilizing smell of garlic and clove will have you nostrils begging for you to take a bite.

Classic ingrediants including tomatoes, red pepper, garlic, cloves, and many others make this recipe simple and easy to repeat. Not too complex to overwhelm the palate but enough to keep your tastebuds dancing.

Ingredients

  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablesppons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Pinch of red pepper
  • Handful of basil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 pound spaghetti

Steps

  1. Add water and a pinch of salt to a pot and spaghetti noodles.
  2. Bring water to a boil and cook noodles until al dente.
  3. Combine tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, red pepper, and basil in separate pot.
  4. Cook until done and then combine.
