Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This is your classic pizza topped with pepperoni. This will bring you back to your childhood.

Keeping it simple with pepperoni, bread, sauce, and cheese. Nothing beats this OG combination.

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pizza Dough
  • Marinera Sauce

Steps

  1. Layout your dough and spread the red sauce.
  2. Heat oven to 350 degrees and add pizza.
  3. After 10 minutes remove pizza from oven and add cheese and pepperoni.
  4. Cook pizza for 20 minutes for perfection.
